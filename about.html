Hi! I’m Eliakim, an artist and graphic designer with a love for creativity in all its forms. It all started with drawing and painting—getting lost in colours, textures, and endless possibilities—which led me to graphic design, where I found new ways to bring ideas to life.

Currently, I’m pursuing a Bachelor of Arts (Honours) in Graphic Design at MCAST, refining my skills and expanding my creative perspective. I’m always exploring—whether it’s branding, packaging, UI/UX, or even art beyond the digital space. I’m driven by the excitement of learning and experimenting.

I believe that great design is more than just visuals—it’s about storytelling, connection, and impact. My goal is to create meaningful, effective, and fun designs that resonate with people.
