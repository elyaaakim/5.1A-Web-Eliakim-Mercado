The Meadow – Branding
Tools: Adobe Illustrator

A space created for artists and like-minded individuals. The café offers a peaceful escape for creativity to thrive, with a serene, nature-inspired atmosphere that encourages relaxation and connection over tea, coffee, and delicious treats.The logo reflects the artistic vibe of the café, combining elements of nature and creativity to represent a space where inspiration and comfort coexist.
