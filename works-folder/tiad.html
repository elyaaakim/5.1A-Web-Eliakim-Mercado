Children’s Storybook – Illustration
Tools: Adobe Indesign, Clip Studio Paint

An 8-page storybook designed for children, created for a school project in Layout Design. The narrative follows a young girl who feels anxious about her future and uncertain about her career path, guided by a conversation with a teacher.
This project focused on layout, storytelling, and print design, with a final printed version produced at the end of the semester.
