Jp Transport – Branding
Tools: Adobe Illustrator

For this project, I worked on creating a new visual identity for a company specializing in transport routes between Poland and Malta. The goal was to strengthen the brand’s presence and highlight its commitment to reliable service.The design uses royal blue and white to convey trust, professionalism, and precision—reflecting the company’s dedication to smooth and dependable cross-border logistics.
