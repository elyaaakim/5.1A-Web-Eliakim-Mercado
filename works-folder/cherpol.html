Cherpol – Visual Identity Rebrand
Tools: Adobe Illustrator

For this project, I redesigned the visual identity for Cherpol, a local menswear brand known for its high-end clothing. The goal was to elevate the brand’s image to reflect a more luxurious and sophisticated standard.
I focused on refining key elements such as the logo, typography, and colour palette, ensuring they aligned with the brand’s focus on elegance and exclusivity.
