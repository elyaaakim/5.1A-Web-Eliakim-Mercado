Blood Donation Campaign
Tools: Clip Studio Paint, Canva

In collaboration with the National Blood Transfusion Service, we were tasked to design a campaign encouraging blood donation. I created illustrations in Clip Studio Paint and designed multiple print layouts in Canva to suit different promotional needs. This project allowed me to explore the fundamentals of illustration and layout design while supporting a meaningful cause.
