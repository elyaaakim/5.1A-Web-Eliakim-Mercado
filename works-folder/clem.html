Character Mascot Design
Tools: Adobe Illustrator

Clem is a cheerful mascot character designed to promote composting and organic gardening in a fun and approachable way for kids. With a bright and friendly design, Clem makes environmental awareness more engaging and relatable, helping children understand the importance of sustainability through play and curiosity. The character aims to inspire young audiences to take small, meaningful actions toward a greener, healthier planet.
