Personal Art
Tools: Clip Studio Paint, Paint Tool SAI

Before studying graphic design, I was deeply invested in digital illustration, focusing on portraiture and human anatomy. Growing up with anime as a major influence, I developed a unique style that combines expressive character work with stylized forms.
This early passion for illustration continues to shape my creative direction today, informing both my artistic and design work.
